Why help us


Pros

  • If you have never created games before that's something you can put in your CV
  • You can work at your own pace. Even quit whenever you want. Just tell us before you do so.
  • We are friendly , we just want people to help us make our dream come true

Cons

  • You won't get paid at the beginning but once we make enough money off of the game you will.
  • I don't know , you tell me.
  • We will work only on discord , we will never see each other IRL

Rules to apply


Rule #1

You must be at least 18 years old

Rule #2

You must live in Europe otherwise it'll be hard to work at the same time

Rule #3

If you are no longer available to work on Mutated, please inform me. Please don't vanish without saying a word. just let us know that we shouldn't bother waiting for you to finish whatever task you were assigned with.

If you want to help Rozalina340 you must send us an example of your previous work. We need serious programmers not kids who think they know programming when they only write a variable.
